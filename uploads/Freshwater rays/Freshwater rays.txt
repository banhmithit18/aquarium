<p>(Potamotrygon falkneri)</p>

<p><strong>Falconer freshwater rays</strong>, also known as&nbsp;<strong>spotted freshwater rays</strong>, belong to the family of&nbsp;<strong>freshwater rays</strong>. This species, which can be found in northeastern Argentina, central Paraguay and western Brazil, prefer to swim near the waterbed and can be rarely seen in open waters.</p>
