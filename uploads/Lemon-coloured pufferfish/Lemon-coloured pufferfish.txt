<p>(Arothron nigropunctatus citrinellus)</p>

<p>The pufferfish family consists of two subfamilies with a total of 26 genera and 190 valid species. The&nbsp;<strong>lemon-coloured pufferfish&nbsp;</strong>belongs to the arothron genus with 15 species. Its circulation area extends over the Indian Ocean and the Pacific, from the coast of Eastern and Southern Africa to Japan, Easter Island and Panama. They live in coral-rich, shallow areas above the 25-metre mark there.</p>
