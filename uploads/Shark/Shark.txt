<p>(Carcharhinus melanopterus)</p>

<p>The name &#39;blacktip reef shark&#39; comes from its black-coloured dorsal fin tip. With a length of up to two metres, it belongs to the group of medium-sized sharks and impressively does its rounds around the shark tank of Aquarium Berlin with, among others, Caribbean nurse sharks (Ginglymostoma cirratum).</p>
