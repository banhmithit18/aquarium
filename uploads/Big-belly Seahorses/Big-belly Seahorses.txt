<p><strong>Big-belly Seahorses</strong>, which are also known as pot-bellied seahorses, grow to a maximum length of 35 cm. Their average length of about 18 to 28 cm makes them the largest seahorse species in the world. Not only their heavily developed body and the two horns on their head are typical of these creatures, the colourful body colouration, ranging from brown, greyish-yellow, orange to white, is also characteristic.</p>
